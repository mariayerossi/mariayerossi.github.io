“MusicPlayer” Music Streaming Website

MusicPlayer is a music streaming website that offers access to millions of songs from various genres and artists over the internet. By utilizing a subscription-based business model, MusicPlayer provides free access to music and other audio content with ads in between songs, while Premium subscribers can enjoy an ad-free experience. Additionally, MusicPlayer also provides an opportunity for artists to upload their music on this website.

Class : Pengembangan Aplikasi Bisnis Berbasis Internet
